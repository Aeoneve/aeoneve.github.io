About Me

My first foray into college began in 2006, earning me a Bachelor of Arts degree in English. After working at EMSL Analytical, Inc. for a number of years, I began taking mechanical engineering classes online at my local community college part-time. Soon afterward, I transferred to Temple University and enrolled full-time, earning my Bachelor of Science degree in Mechancial Engineering while earning a minor in computer science. The following year, I earned my Master of Science degree in Mechanical Engineering. I was hired the same year by the Department of the Navy as an Engineer, where I am still currently working. Presently, I am working toward my Master of Science degree in Computer Science utilizing the remote learning program at Old Dominion University.
